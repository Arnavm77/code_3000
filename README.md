# Personal Ethics Statement

Ethics is doing the right thing even when it doesn't feel like the easiest option. I am driven
by curiosity and innovation, but I know that technology without accountability can
cause real harm. People deserve to understand and trust the systems that affect their
lives, and that starts with the people who build them.

I try to ask not just whether a solution works, but whether it is fair and considerate
of who it will impact. I take responsibility for my decisions and stay honest about what
I know and do not know. I also look for ways to support others who are newer to the field.

Ethics is something I practice every day. I hold myself accountable by regularly 
reflecting on whether my actions match my values, seeking out perspectives different from my own, 
and pushing back when something feels wrong, rather than ignoring it for convenience. 
The goal is to prioritize people in everyday life and in technology consistently.
